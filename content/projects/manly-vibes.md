## The Brief

Northern Beaches Council sought a utility artwork that could transform a functional infrastructure surface into a celebration of Manly's identity — its surf culture, coastal ecology, and community spirit.

## Community Engagement

Drawing on his lifelong connection to the Northern Beaches as a surfer, freediver, and surf lifesaver, Patrick brought a deeply personal understanding to the project. Community input highlighted the importance of marine life, the iconic Manly to Shelly Beach walk, and the laid-back coastal culture that defines the area.

## Process

Manly Vibes brings together ocean creatures, native plants, surf culture iconography, and the flowing energy of the Pacific Ocean into a cohesive, joyful composition. The colour palette mirrors the Northern Beaches — deep ocean blues, sandy golds, and the vivid greens of coastal banksia.

## Outcome

The mural won the Utility Award at the 2019 Australian Street Art Awards, establishing InkHunter as a significant voice in Australian public art. The artwork continues to be one of the most photographed and shared pieces of street art on the Northern Beaches.
